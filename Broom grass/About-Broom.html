Chamarel

Everything begins in Chamarel, where my grandparents grew up. Chamarel is a small village in Mauritius' southwest. The Seven Colored Earths, Chamarel Falls, Ebony Forest Chamarel, and the Black River Gorges National Park are among the places known for their beautiful nature. The surrounding area is also well-known for its coffee, which is grown locally. We also have a curious corner, which is a house of illusion and mystery that is sure to pique your interest

Boom Grass Plant

This is a broom grass plantation that takes 4 to 5 years to grow. However, once harvested, broom grass takes about a year to reproduce.

Harvested Broom

When it comes time to harvest, their children and friends will pitch in.

They will cut the broom grass edge to the same length.

After all of this, they will expose the broom grass to the sun. In order for them to dry.

Here is a vacoas plant that will be used to make the broom.

They will do the same thing as the broom, expose it to the sun to dry.
