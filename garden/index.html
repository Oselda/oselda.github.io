Garden

A garden is a lovely addition to any home. Because it is the only place where a person can get away from their hectic lifestyle.
Having a garden in your home has numerous health benefits. A garden contains a variety of plants that produce oxygen. Plants
in my garden include roses, mangoes, golden apple, hibiscus, and others. These flowers are the easiest to grow and benefit the
environment with their lovely scents. The colors of these flowers add a lovely touch to a garden. My grandparents and my father are
the one who take care of the garden. They are a nature lover. They take time out of their busy schedules to care for the garden.
